CA
The CA exam, administered by the ICAI, assesses candidates for Chartered Accountancy in India, covering subjects like accounting, taxation, and auditing through rigorous stages: Foundation, Intermediate, and Final examinations.


CLAT
The CLAT exam, or Common Law Admission Test, is a national entrance test for law programs in India, assessing candidates on English, mathematics, legal reasoning, and general knowledge.


CSEET
The CSEET, or Company Secretary Executive Entrance Test, evaluates candidates aspiring to become company secretaries in India, testing knowledge in business communication, legal aptitude, and current affairs through objective and subjective questions.

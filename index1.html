NEET
NEET is a national entrance exam for aspiring medical students in India. It assesses knowledge in physics, chemistry, and biology, determining eligibility for undergraduate medical and dental programs nationwide.


ICAR
The ICAR exam is a national-level entrance test for admission to undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in agricultural and allied sciences across various Indian universities


BHU
The BHU exam, conducted by Banaras Hindu University, evaluates candidates for various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, testing knowledge in subjects like science, arts, and commerce through objective and descriptive formats.

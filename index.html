JEE
The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) is a National-level engineering entrance exam for admission to IITs, NITs and IIITs testing Physics, Chemistry and Methematics knowledge and problem solving skills.


IISER
The Indian Institute of Science Education and research exam is a national leve entrance test for admission to IISERs assessing Science and Mathematics knowledge for undergraduate programs.


BITSAT
BITSAT is an online entrance exam for admission to BITS Pilani and its campuses. It tests proficiency in physics, chemistry, mathematics, and English, assessing problem-solving and analytical skills.


NEST
NEST is a national entrance test for admission to integrated M.Sc. programs in premier institutions. It assesses knowledge in biology, chemistry, mathematics, and physics, focusing on critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
